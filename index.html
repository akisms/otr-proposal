Automation Proposal

PR Intelligence System, powered by automation.

A fully automated platform that monitors news, scores articles to your preferences, generates intelligence briefings with audio on your chosen cadence, and writes executive content in their voice.

The big picture

Four things this system does for you, on autopilot.

01

Finds the news

Monitors your chosen RSS sources 24/7 and pulls in every new article automatically.

02

Reads and ranks it

AI reads each article and scores it 0–100 based on what matters to you. It learns your preferences over time.

03

Creates your briefing

On your chosen cadence — daily or weekly — you get a themed intelligence briefing as a document and a podcast you can listen to.

04

Writes for your execs

Learns each executive's writing voice and generates LinkedIn posts, op-eds, and memos that sound like them.

Capabilities

What the system can do.

Automated RSS article ingestion

Monitors feeds, extracts full article text, and generates AI summaries for every new article.

Personalized scoring

AI scores every article 0–100 based on your preferences, with both manual configuration and adaptive learning from your feedback.

Daily intelligence briefings

Automated and on-demand themed briefings with grouped articles, trend analysis, and a watch list.

Podcast generation

Briefings automatically converted to natural-sounding audio podcasts via ElevenLabs text-to-speech.

Executive voice profiling

Submit writing samples and get an instant AI-generated voice profile capturing tone, vocabulary, structure, and signature phrases.

Ghostwriting with article context

Generate on-brand content in any executive's voice, optionally linked to a specific high-scoring article for contextual relevance.

Custom briefings on demand

Request briefings with specific date ranges, score thresholds, source filters, and article limits.

Adaptive preference learning

After 5+ article ratings, the system infers your preferences and automatically updates scoring criteria.

Automation layer

Ten modular Zaps.

Each automation is independent and editable. They connect through data tables.

RSS → Articles

Trigger: RSS by Zapier — New Item in Feed
Frequency: Per new feed item
Trigger
RSS feed detects new item
Headline, URL, publish date, and source extracted.
Step 2
GET request to article URL
Retrieves raw HTML content from the article's web page.
Step 3 · AI
Claude extracts text and summarizes
Cleans HTML, removes ads/nav, generates 100–150 word summary.
Step 4
Create record in Articles table
Title, URL, Source, Full_Text, AI_Summary, Status=“unscored”.
Result: New article in database, ready for scoring.

Article Scoring

Trigger: New unscored article in Articles table
Frequency: Immediate
Trigger
New article with Status=“unscored”
Fires when Zap 1 creates a new article record.
Step 2
Get Preference_Profile from Settings
Retrieves single-row Settings table with all scoring instructions.
Step 3 · AI
Claude scores the article
Scores relevance 0–100. Outputs overall_score, topic_scores JSON, and rationale.
Step 4
Update article record
Writes Score, Topic_Scores, Rationale. Sets Status=“scored”.
Result: Article scored and ready for briefing or review.

Adaptive Preference Learning

Trigger: Article feedback updated
Threshold: ≥5 ratings required
Trigger
User rates article as “Relevant” or “Not Relevant”
Fires when User_Feedback field is updated. Optional Relevancy_Notes provide additional context.
Step 2
Find 10 most recent rated articles
Searches articles with feedback and relevancy notes, sorted by timestamp.
Step 3
Filter: continue only if ≥5 ratings
Prevents learning from insufficient data.
Step 4 · AI
Claude infers preferences from patterns and notes
Analyzes feedback ratings and relevancy notes to understand why certain topics matter. Generates updated scoring rules.
Step 5
Update Settings table
Writes inferred Preference_Profile. Future articles scored with learned criteria.
Result: System adapts to your preferences automatically.

Scheduled Intelligence Briefing

Trigger: Schedule — Daily or Weekly
Frequency: Configurable
Trigger
Schedule fires on chosen cadence
Configurable cadence (daily or weekly), time, and timezone.
Step 2
Find high-scoring articles from last 24h
Filters: Score ≥50, published within last 24 hours, max 25 articles.
Step 3 · AI
Claude generates themed briefing
Groups by theme, highlights trends, includes watch list, references sources. Outputs markdown.
Step 4
Create Briefings record
Date, Title, Markdown_Summary stored. Triggers Zap 5 for audio.
Result: Daily briefing created. Podcast generation starts automatically.

Podcast Generator

Trigger: New record in Briefings table
Frequency: After Zap 4 or 8
Trigger
New briefing record created
Fires for both daily (Zap 4) and custom (Zap 8) briefings.
Step 2 · AI
Claude converts to podcast script
Transforms markdown into natural narration with [PAUSE] markers.
Step 3
Split script on [PAUSE] markers
Creates array of text chunks for individual TTS processing.
Step 4
Send each chunk to ElevenLabs
Loop: generates MP3 audio for each chunk via TTS API.
Step 5
Combine audio and update briefing
Merges into single MP3. Writes Audio_URL back to Briefings table.
Result: Briefing available as both text and audio.

Ghostwriter

Trigger: Manual or form trigger
Frequency: On-demand
Trigger
User requests draft
Executive name, format (LinkedIn post, op-ed, etc.), and optional topic.
Step 2
Get executive's Voice_Profile
Looks up Executives table for AI-generated voice guide.
Step 3 · AI
Claude generates content in exec's voice
Uses Voice_Profile + Format + context to produce polished, on-brand draft.
Step 4
Create draft record
Executive, Format, AI_Draft, Status=“draft”.
Result: Draft ready for executive review.

Executive Profile Generator

Trigger: New Exec Profile Request
Frequency: Immediate
Trigger
New executive onboarding form submitted
Record created with Status=“profiled”. No pending state.
Step 2 · AI
Claude analyzes writing samples
Extracts tone, vocabulary, structure, signature phrases, key themes.
Step 3
Create Executives record
Name + Voice_Profile stored. Ready for ghostwriting.
Step 4
Update request with generated profile
Writes Generated_Voice_Profile back to Exec Requests for audit.
Result: Executive profile available for ghostwriting in ~30 seconds.

Custom Briefing Generator

Trigger: Briefing Generator Form
Frequency: On-demand
Trigger
Form submission received
Title, Start_Date, End_Date, Minimum_Score, Source_Filter, Article_Limit.
Step 2
Parse form inputs
Extracts all filter parameters.
Step 3
Dynamic query against Articles
Filters by date range, min score, source, article limit. Sorted by score.
Step 4 · AI
Claude generates themed briefing
Groups, trends, watch list from filtered articles.
Step 5
Create Briefings record
User-provided title. Zap 5 triggers automatically for audio.
Result: Custom briefing ready in 2–3 minutes (text + audio).

Scoring Preferences

Trigger: Scoring Preferences Form
Frequency: On-demand
Trigger
Form submission received
Content_Priorities, Content_Dislikes, Industry_Focus, Tone_Preferences.
Step 2
Parse and validate fields
Extracts all four preference categories.
Step 3
Combine into Preference_Profile
Structures into markdown with priorities, dislikes, focus areas, scoring rules.
Step 4
Update Settings table
Zap 2 uses new criteria immediately for next article scored.
Result: Scoring criteria take effect within seconds. Works alongside Zap 3.

Ghostwriting Request Handler

Trigger: Ghostwriting Request Form
Frequency: On-demand
Trigger
Form submission received
Executive (dropdown), Content Format, optional Article Context (linked record, Score ≥50, last 7 days).
Step 2
Parse inputs and check article context
Determines whether an article was linked. Branches into two paths.
Step 3
Get Voice_Profile + Article (if linked)
Retrieves executive profile from Executives table. If article selected, also gets Title and Full_Text.
Step 4 · AI
Claude generates context-aware content
With article: Content referencing the specific article. Without: General on-brand content in exec's voice.
Step 5
Create draft record
Executive, Format, AI_Draft, Status=“draft”. Notification sent to user.
Result: Context-aware draft ready for review. Linked articles produce more topical content.

Key workflows

End-to-end flows.

How the system operates from user action to final output.
Setup

User fills Scoring Preferences Form

Zap 9 updates Settings. Optionally, user submits Executive Onboarding Form.

Zap 1

New RSS article detected and ingested

Claude extracts text and summarizes. Article added with Status=“unscored”.

Zap 2

Article scored using Preference_Profile

Score 0–100, topic scores, and rationale generated.

Zap 4 + 5

Briefing generated on your chosen cadence with podcast

Top articles grouped by theme. Cadence set to daily or weekly. Audio version created via ElevenLabs.

Feedback

User rates articles and adds notes, Zap 3 learns

Rate articles as relevant or not, with optional notes explaining why. After 5+ ratings, preferences auto-update.

User action

“I need a briefing for AI articles from last week”

Fills Briefing Generator Form with title, date range, filters.

Zap 8

Dynamic query and briefing generation

Filters articles, Claude creates themed markdown briefing.

Zap 5

Audio version generated automatically

Script converted to podcast via ElevenLabs.

Result

Custom briefing ready in 2–3 minutes

Available as text and audio in the Briefings table.

User action

“Generate a LinkedIn post about AI governance for Jane Smith”

Fills Ghostwriting Request Form. Optionally links a scored article.

Zap 10

Voice_Profile retrieved, content generated

Claude uses Jane's tone, vocabulary, and structure preferences. If article linked, content references it.

Result

Draft ready for Jane to review

Stored in Drafts table. Status: draft → reviewed → approved.

User action

Onboard a new executive

Fills Executive Onboarding Form with name, writing samples, content types.

Zap 7

Claude analyzes writing samples

Extracts tone, vocabulary, structure, signature phrases, key themes.

Result

Profile ready for ghostwriting in ~30 seconds

Executive added to system. Available for Zap 6/10 content generation.

Getting started

What we need from you.

We deliver all Zaps, tables, and forms as ready-to-use templates. Here's what your team provides to get the system running.
1

RSS feeds to monitor

A list of news sources, trade publications, or blogs you want the system to track. These can be added or changed at any time.

2

Initial scoring preferences

What topics matter most, what to deprioritize, industry focus areas, and any specific keywords or themes. Submitted via the Scoring Preferences Form.

3

Briefing cadence and schedule

How often you want briefings (daily or weekly), what time, and which timezone.

4

Executive writing samples

For each executive who needs ghostwritten content: 3–5 writing samples (articles, blog posts, LinkedIn posts) submitted via the Executive Onboarding Form.

5

API credentials

An Anthropic Claude API key and an ElevenLabs API key. We'll walk you through setup if needed.
